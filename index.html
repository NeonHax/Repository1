Children's Ministry

Children are a blessing from the Lord. Churches have a responsibility toward children, too. We want to provide an environment in which children are safe, are taught at an age-appropriate level, and are encouraged to develop a relationship with Jesus Christ.

Praise And Worship

“O magnify the Lord with me, and let us exalt His name together” (Psalm 34:3). A music team that exalts the name of the Lord having their main goal: To worship and bless the name of our God and make Him stand on His throne.
